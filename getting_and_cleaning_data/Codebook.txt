CODEBOOK for Course project of "Getting and Cleaning data" on-line course

"Activities" - STRING
	Types of performed tasks by subjects.

	Values can be one of the following:
	- WALKING
	- WALKING_UPSTAIRS
	- WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
	- SITTING
	- STANDING
	- LAYING

"Volunteers" - INTEGER
	Subjects who performed the tasks listed under "Activities"
	
	Value range: 1-30 ( inclusive )
